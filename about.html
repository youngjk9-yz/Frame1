About Us

Our members span the globe, united by a shared mission to empower women in leadership, career growth, and community building.

Our Story

The Frame Foundation began with a simple idea: women grow faster when they grow together.

We saw talented women navigating leadership, career transitions, and new challenges—often without a strong support system or community to lean on.

We created The Frame Foundation to change that. Today, we bring together women from around the world to learn, connect, and support one another through shared experiences and meaningful conversations.

Our vision is to build a global community where women are not only advancing in their careers, but also helping others rise along the way.

Core Values

Growth

We believe growth is ongoing. We create space for learning, reflection, and continuous development.

Leadership

Leadership is not a title—it’s impact. We support women in leading with confidence and purpose.

Network

Strong connections open doors. We foster relationships that extend beyond events and across borders.

Mentorship

We grow by lifting others. We encourage guidance, support, and shared knowledge within our community.

Our Impact

We create space for women to grow into leadership, build strong networks, and support one another through shared experiences.

Our community continues to evolve through regular meetings, webinars, and meaningful connections across borders.
